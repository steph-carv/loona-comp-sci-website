Who Is Loona?

MEMBERS

Loona is a 12 member korean girl group previously under Blockberry Creative Entertainment. Containing members Heejin, Hyunjin, Haseul, Yeojin, Vivi, Kim Lip Jinsoul, Yves, Chuu, Gowon, and Olivia Hye they debuted in a unique fashion that garnered them lots of attention. Coupling this with their intrguing lore they were able to attract many fans throughout the globe. But really who is loona, and what story did they have to tell? The first girl to debut was Heejin. Heejin brought colour to a once grey and gloomy world in her MV, for not just her but for 4 others, Hyunjin, Haseul, and Vivi. These 4 girls (also known as loona 1/3) where from the "real world". The next set of girls to debut where Kim Lip, Jinsoul and Choerry. These three girls (also known as loona odd eye circle) lived between the "real world" and eden, and each helf a special power given to them by their odd eye. The last 4 to debut, Yves, Chuu, Gowon, and Olivia Hye (also known as yyxy) where from Eden. With the help of OEC, yyxy where able to escape eden and reunite with the 12 girls.

On 25th of November 2022, Loonas former company kicked member Chuu from the group. This resulted in a domino effect in which the other 11 girls filed for contract terminations. On June 16th all members had officially won their termination lawsuits, and where now to move on to a new chapter. 5 members, Heejin, Haseul, Kim Lip, Jinsoul, and Choerry joined MODHAUS and are currently under the name ARTMS. 5 members, Hyunjin, Yeojin, Vivi, Gowon and Olivia Hye, joined CTD Ent. and are under the group name Loossemble. Chuu and Yves have both decided to go solo, with Chuu's redebut on the 18th of October. The story of Loona, both in their lore and in the real world is striking and too long for me to go into depth, but it is above all a story of friendship.
